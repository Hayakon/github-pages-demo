Michelin’s 5 pillars of data quality:

1. Accuracy – data reflects reality

2. Consistency – data is aligned across the organization

3. Completeness – missing data is promptly identified

4. Timeliness – data is regularly updated

5. Security & Compliance – data is protected according to GDPR and corporate standards
